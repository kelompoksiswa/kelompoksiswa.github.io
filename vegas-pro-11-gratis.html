periode getaran
T = 2π √(m / k)
T = 2π √(4×10⁻⁴ / 10)
T = 2π √4×10⁻⁵
T = 2π • (1/500)√10
T = (π/250)√10 ≈ 0,04 s ✔️

frekuensi
f = 1/T
