27. Change into Passive Sentences (20) 1. The master has given the orders. 2. The AB steered the vessel last night. 3. They paint the hull of the ship. 4. General cargo Ship is carrying general cargoes.​


Jawaban:The orders have been given by the masterThe vessel was steered by The AB last night The hull of the ship is painted by them General cargoes is being carried by General Cargo

PEMBAHASAN

Change into Passive Sentences

=> Ubahlah kalimat menjadi kalimat Passive

Berdasarkan Rumus

A. Present Perfect Active: S + has/have + V3 + OPassive: O + has/have + been + V3 + By S

1. The master has given the orders.

=> the orders have been given by the master

B. Past Tense Active: S + V2 + OPassive: O + Be (was/were) + V3 + By S

2. The AB steered the vessel last night.

=> The vessel was steered by The AB last night

C. Present Tense Active: S + V1 (-s/es) + OPassive: O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By s

3. They paint the hull of the ship.

=> The hull of the ship is painted by them

D. Present Continuous Active: S + is/am/are + V-ing + OPassive: O + is/am/are + Being + V3 + By S

4. General cargo Ship is carrying general cargoes.

=> General cargoes is being carried by General Cargo
