18. apa kritik dari film snow white dalam bahasa inggris


When using the perspective of feminist criticism of literature based on the sexual politics context of Kate Millet, it is seen that the figure of Snow White and Queen is an artificial "woman" from a male perspective. Snow White is a figure of a woman who needs help and with the help of Hunters, 7 dwarves, and the Prince, she finally gets away from the Queen's evil plans. Instead the Queen is an independent female figure who is able to regulate himself. Unfortunately, he must get the impression of evil and eventually die pathetic. The Snow White tale that has a moral message that goodness will prevail against evil shows that the Queen who has evil will reap the disastrous of what she does even though she is beautiful, knowledgeable, and powerful.
