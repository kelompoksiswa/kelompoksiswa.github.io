30. Make simple past tense with;definition,pattern and five samples


Simple Past TensePembahasan ● Definition

Simple Past Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu Kegiatan di masa lalu atau Peristiwa yang terjadi di masa lampau. Ciri waktu lampau:

YesterdayLast nightLast week/ month/ YearTwo days agoTwo week ago

● Pattern

1. Verbal Sentence: Kalimat Verba karena ada kata kerja (verb) di dalam kalimatnya

(+) S + Verb 2 + O + Adverb (-) S + did not + Verb 1 + O + Adverb (?) Did + S + Verb 1 + O + Adverb

2. Nominal Sentence: Kalimat Nomina karena tidak ada kata kerja di dalamnya.

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + Adj/Adv/Noun(-) S + Tobe (was/were) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun(?) Tobe (was/were) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun?

=> Subjek + Tobe

I, he, She, it + wasYou, we, they + were

● Five samples

1.

(+) I cooked fried rice yesterday(-) I did not cook fried rice yesterday(?) Did I cook fried rice yesterday?

2.

(+) you played the piano two days ago(-) you did not play the piano two days ago(?) Did you play the piano two days ago?

3.

(+) They studied English last week (-) They did not study English last week (?) Did They study English last week?

4.

(+) You were a short last year(-) You were not a short last year(?) were you a short last year ?

5.

(+) We were sad last night(-) We were not sad last night(?) were We sad last night?

Catatan

No. 1, 2 dan 3 menggunakan rumus verbal sentenceNo. 4 dan 5 menggunakan rumus nominal sentence
