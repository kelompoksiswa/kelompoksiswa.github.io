18. Dik:P=500 PAA=50.000 CM2DIT:F=...?​


Diketahui:

» P = 500 Pa

» A = 50.000 cm²

Ditanya:

F (Force/Gaya)?

Jawab:

- Rumus Tekanan:

P = F ÷ A

Dimana:

» P = Pressure/Tekanan (N/m², Pa)

» F = Force/Gaya (N)

» A = Area/Luas bidang (m²)

- Rumus mencari F (Gaya):

P = F ÷ A

F = P × A

———

- Ubah dulu cm² ke m²

= 50.00 cm²

= (50.000 ÷ 10.000) m²

= 5 m²

- Maka, besarnya Gaya:

= P × A

= 500 × 5 N

= [2.500 N]

Pembuktian:

P = F ÷ A

P = 2.500 N ÷ 5 m²

P = 500 Pa → terbukti
