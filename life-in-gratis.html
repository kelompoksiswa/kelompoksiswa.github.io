6. You/life/ in this town all your life masukkan ke dalam present perfect 2​


Bentuk kalimat present perfect dari "you/life/in this town all your life" adalah...

Pembahasan :

=> Karena yang diminta adalah bentuk present perfect, maka rumus yang digunakan adalah :

S + Have/Has + V3 + O

Dan karena subject yang digunakan "you", maka kita menggunakan auxiliary verb "Have". Berikut ini adalah kalimat nya :

=> You HAVE LIVED in this town all your life.
=====================
Mapel : Bahasa Inggris
Materi : Present Perfect
Kelas : 10
Kode Soal : 10.5
Kode Kategorisasi : 10.5.4

1. Cut Nyak Dhien was a leader of the Acehnese guerrilla forces
2. Cut Nyak Dhien was exiled to Sumedang, West Java, because the afraid of the Dutch.
Struggle Cut Nyak Dien - Acehnese Heroes The struggle of Cut Nyak Dien against the Dutch and expelling the Dutch colonial government from Tanah Rencong is so persistent and relentless. Cut Nyak Dien's struggle increasingly made the Dutch colonial government overwhelmed by it. The battle tactics are so ingenious. A woman who is so intelligent, courageous and loving towards the Earth Pertiwi.Kota Aceh is one city that has a lot of history. One of the historical heritages is the Baiturrahman Mosque. This is the icon of Aceh, which is the pride of the people of Serambi Mecca, the Baiturrahman Mosque. This magnificent mosque is not only to worship the Muslims, but this mosque is a proof of concrete documentation about the early entry of Islam to Aceh brought by Gujarat merchants. Baiturrahman Mosque also confirmed that Aceh as the Veranda of Mecca Indonesia.Masjid was built in the 17th century, during the era of the Sultanate of Iskandar Muda. Initially, this mosque is a sultanate mosque, but as Islam spread in Aceh, this mosque was opened to the public. The mosque has undergone several extensions and renovations, including witnessing the history of Indonesia's independence struggle, including the struggle of a Cut Nyak Dien. The struggle of Cut Nyak DienBeliau was born in Lampadang, Aceh, in 1850. His father and husband were freedom fighters. When the Dutch occupied his homeland, he took refuge and parted with his father and husband. This separation became the end of his meeting with his beloved husband. Teuku Ibrahim Lamnga, her husband, fell in battle with the Dutch in Gletarum, June 1878. Cut Nyak Dien did not accept the humiliation of the Dutch colonial government that initially attacked Aceh and destroyed the place of worship. His anger toward the Dutch was increasingly becoming the moment when his first husband, Teuku Cek Ibrahim Lamnga, died in the war. Auction of 2 years after the death of her husband, she remarried with one of the great warriors named Teuku Umar. Cut Nyak Dien was proposed by an Acehnese warrior named Teuku Umar. Together with her second husband, Cut Nyak Dien increasingly eager to expel the Dutch invaders. In order to defend the territory and independence, Cut Nyak Dien was not afraid to go to war against the Dutch who possessed sophisticated weaponry. But unfortunately, the tragic story that happened once again happened again. Teuku Umar died in battle in Meulaboh on February 11, 1899. After her husband Teuku Umar died, Cut Nyak Dien continued his struggle alone. However, he was not upset. No less mental despite being left beloved husband who died in the battlefield. Cut Nyak Dien continued to strike at the Dutch headquarters with his followers. Cut Nyak Dien became the most wanted man by the Dutch to be killed because his struggle threatened the existence and continuity of the Dutch colonial government in the Veranda of Mecca. However, Cut Nyak Dien's struggle was betrayed by his men, Pang Lot, who informed the Dutch where Cut Nyak Dien.Cut Nyak Dien's hideout was then old and blind because his eyes had acute agony, could not escape further from the Dutch attack that arrived -because. He was eventually arrested and taken to Banda Aceh. Then, exiled to Sumedang, West Java. Cut Nyak Dien struggle must be stopped because he was detained in Sumedang.Di in custody, he was nicknamed as Ibu Perbu because Cut Nyak Dien so understand the teachings of religion. A scholar named Ilyas who was also arrested gave him the nickname. On November 6, 1906, Cut Nyak Dien's struggle really ended with his return to the Creator. He was buried in Sumedang and his grave was only discovered in 1959 on the orders of Aceh governor Ali Hasan. Cut Nyak Dien's food search was based on data found in the Netherlands. Thanks to his unyielding spirit, he was named a national hero by Presidential Decree No. RI. 106 of 1964 on May 2, 1964. The struggle of Cut Nyak Dien in realizing Indonesian independence inspired the director Eros Djarot to immortalize it in a film in 1988. Christine Hakim was asked to act as Cut Nyak Dien. The film Tjoet Nja 'Dhien was awarded the Citra Cup as the best film category and the film became the first Indonesian production film to be screened at the Cannes Film Festival. The struggle of Cut Nyak Dien did not stop with his death. The struggle of this nation's heroes will continue to grow, live, and lasting, in the soul of the Indonesian nation, especially the people of Aceh.Sumber: Museum of Heroes
