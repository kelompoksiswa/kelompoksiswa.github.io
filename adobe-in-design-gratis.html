4. What is student problem in preparing the course design​


Jawaban:

∴ Preparing for the course design:

Course design is the process and methodology of creating quality learning environments and experiences for students. Through deliberate and structured expose to instructional materials, learning activities, and interaction, students are able to access information, obtain skills, and practice higher levels of thinking.

Problem in preparing course design:

1) Creating a quality environment

example: make an clean and comfortable class for your student.

2) Creating an interactive course

example: make an course that isn't boring but fun and interactive.

3) Create an socialable course

example: give an opportunity for your students to interact each other so that they can solve a problem together.

4) Give many opportunity for students to try

example: look for real examples in everyday life that students can solve independently.

5) Always open for feedback

example: always listen to the students for what they think about the course.

Adobe After Effects merupakan salah satu aplikasi yang didesain oleh Adobe. Aplikasi ini awalnya merupakan produk yang dihasilkan Macromedia, akan tetapi sudah menjadi milik Adobe saat ini.

Beberapa aplikasi lain yang masuk dalam jajaran pengolah tata letak dalam dunia desain grafis adalah Adobe FrameMaker dan Corel Ventura.
