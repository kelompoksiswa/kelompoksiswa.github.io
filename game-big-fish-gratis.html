2. Many small fish are swimming in that big pond. In negative form is .... A.Many small fish is not swimming in that big pond.B.Many small fish are now swimming in that big pond.C.Many small fish is swimming in that big pond.D.Many small fish aren't swimming in that big pond.#Mohon bantuannya kk makasih ​


Jawaban:

D. Many small fish aren't swimming in that big pond.

Penjelasan:

Present Continuous Tense

Untuk bentuknya menjadi negatif, kita lihat dulu subjeknya. Karena subjeknya Many small fish maka subjeknya noun plural atau kata benda jamak. Secara sederhana, kita hanya perlu menambahkan kata not pada auxiliary verb/to be are menjadi are not/aren't.
